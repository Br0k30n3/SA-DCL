About Us

The Department of Certification and Licensing exists to ensure that certain occupations and professions in a state adhere to the legal and ethical requirements set by the state. Their standards ensure a high level of professional competence and ensure public safety by making sure professionals have the necessary skills, knowledge, and experience. Utilizing their resources can help employers and employees maintain compliance with state regulations and maintain a professional standard in their work. Whether you are an individual looking to apply for a license or certification, renew an existing one or an employer seeking to hire certified professionals, the Department of Certification and Licensing should be your first stop. They can provide detailed information on the requirements, timelines, fees, and procedures that apply to your specific certification or licensing. With regular monitoring and enforcement of standards, the Department ensures that the public is protected and that professionals continue to meet the high expectations of their respective industries. Aspiring professionals seeking licensure and certification must navigate a complex and ever-changing regulatory landscape. The Department of Certification and Licensing recognizes this and exists to provide guidance and direction to individuals seeking information on requirements specific to their state. It is important to note that licensing and certification requirements vary state by state, and it is crucial to be aware of the guidelines set forth within your respective governing body. The Department of Certification and Licensing can provide guidance on the specific requirements for your state, including information on application processes, continuing education requirements, and renewal protocols. With the assistance of the Department of Certification and Licensing, individuals seeking licensure can streamline their application process and ensure that they are meeting all necessary criteria.



In conclusion, the Department of Certification and Licensing is an essential authority that oversees the certification and licensing process of various professions and trades. The department plays a crucial role in ensuring public safety by enforcing industry standards and regulations. It also provides individuals with the opportunity to gain credibility and increase their employability through certifications and licenses. As a result, it is essential that individuals and organizations work with the department to ensure compliance and maintain high standards in their respective industries.
